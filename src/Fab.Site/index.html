Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. So, why Silverlight and not HTML 5 for main browser application?

    Silverlight is widely supported on all major browsers like Firefox, Chrome and Internet Explorer on both Windows and Mac platforms and it provide a way to build rich and solid client not only for web, but for desktop as well with the fastest and straightforward deployment model that support automatic updates.

  2. Do you have clients for mobile devices?

    Clients for Windows Phone and Windows RT are in the active development.
    Android and iPhone/iPad are also on our road, just a little further.

  3. Is there any tech support?

    Since nReez is supposed to be completely anonymous, we do not offer personal support, but any general issues or suggestions could be posted on our forum - just make sure that you do not mentioned your nReez login name there.
